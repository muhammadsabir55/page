Nangarhar

Nangarhar is one of the 34 provinces of Afghanistan, located in the East part of the country and bordering Logar , Kabul, Laghman, and kunar provinces as well as having an international border with Pakistan . It is divided into 22 districts and has a population of about 1,735,531 the third highest province of the country. The city of Jalalabad is the capital of Nangarhar province. Nangarhar province is famous for fish and ice cream, the popular tourist location Daroonta and kama.
