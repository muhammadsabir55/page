Wellcome to Online visit of Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a landlocked country located at the crossroads of central Asia and south Asia. Reffered to as the Heart of Asia , it is bordered by Pakistan to the East and south , Iran to the West , Turkmenistan to the Northwest , Uzbekistan to the North, Tajikistan to the northeast and China to the northeast and East. Occupying 652,864 square kilometer of land . Kabul is the country's largest city and serves as its capital . Accordong to the world Population review, as of 2021, Afghanistan Population is 40.2 million .
Afghanistanis divided into 34 provinces. The provinces of Afghanistanare the primary administrative division. Each province encompasses a number of districts or usually over 1000 villages.
