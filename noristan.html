Nooristan

Nooristan is one of the 34 provinces of Afghanistan, located in the East part of the country. It is divided into 7 districts and is a population Afghanistan's least population province, with a populationof around 167000. Parun serves as the provincial capital. Nooristan is bordered on the south by Laghman and Kunar provinces , on the north by Badakhshan province, on the west by Panjshir province.
